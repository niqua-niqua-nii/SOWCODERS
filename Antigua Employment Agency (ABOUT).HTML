ABOUT US

Our Mission

We here at the Antigua Employment Agency, seek to find suitable and rewarding jobs for recent graduates of a tertiary educational program, dependent on the students strengths and qualifications. We also seek to equip places of employments with a solid and capable workforce in order to maximize profit and to help lower unemployment rates.

Why are we so invested in providing jobs for graduates?

Take a look at the video below, which speaks about unemployment rates.

Despite our world becoming more accessible in terms of medicine and technological pursuits, unemployment still persists, especially in younger persons. A recent study indicate that 30% of recent graduates weren't able to find a job within a year a year of graduating, much less one relating to their major. We want to cut the middle man and link these individuals to a job, quickly and efficiently.

At AEA we seek to lower the rates of unemployment island-wide and work forward to a more productive society. Our team is dedicated to finding the best job opportunities and best employees for the individual job-seeker or company.

We understand how hard it is to find a job, especially just coming out of school and we seek to assist in the best way we can.
