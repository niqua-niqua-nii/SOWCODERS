Welcome to the Antigua Employment Agency

Your one stop shop for Post-Graduate Employment.

Our Mission

We here at the Antigua Employment Agency, seek to find suitable and rewarding jobs for recent graduates of a tertiary educational program, dependent on the students strengths and qualifications. We also seek to equip places of employments with a solid and capable workforce in order to maximize profit and to help lower unemployment rates.

When you sign up for the Antigua Employment Agency:

As a student:

  • You have an increased chance of getting a job compatible for your particular strengths
  • We offer jobs only at legitimate and accredited institutions, which meet the labor-code requirements
  • We offer the lowest subscription rate, in comparison to other employment agencies
  • We have the success employment rate on island

As an Employer:

  • Our applicants go through evaluation to ensure that you gain the best staff
  • Many candidates recommended by us have made significant impacts in the businesses they were sent to (See our success stories)
  • With our premium subscription you can get access to our database to look for the criteria that your business need most
